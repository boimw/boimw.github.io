About Me

Bojan Marjanovic was born in Bijeljina, Bosnia and Herzegovina, but currently lives in Munich, Germany. He finished his BSc in Applied Computer Science at the Faculty of Technical Sciences in Novi Sad, Serbia. After graduating from Faculty of Technical Sciences, Bojan continues his Master studies at Free University of Bozen-Bolzano, field of study: Software Engineering, as an Erasmus+ schoolarship holder. After spending one year in Italy and finishing the first year of his Master studies, he moved to Madrid, where he continues the second year of his studies at Universidad Politécnica de Madrid. After graduating from his master studies, he relocates to Munich in order to continue his professional career. Currently, he is a part of software company in Munich, working as lead Ruby on Rails developer.
